# Tier 3 Enriched Handoff — PRE

## Situation before workflow adjustment

The workflow follows a declared path through Intake, Retrieve, Draft, Review, and Finalize. Handoffs occur at stage boundaries, but enriched capture is inconsistent: handoff summaries are missing or thin, owner/receiver roles are implicit, and decision status is not consistently carried into review.

## Observable evidence pattern

- Repeated downstream clarification after Draft → Review handoff.
- Reason codes skew toward clarification rather than decision.
- Open questions reappear at review rather than being listed before transfer.
- Evidence references and decision status are sparse at handoff points.

## Enriched evidence readout

| Evidence field | PRE readout |
|---|---|
| Handoff summary | Missing or thin |
| Owner / receiver | Implicit |
| Decision status | Not consistently carried |
| Open questions | Reopened downstream |
| Reason codes | Clarification-heavy |

## Public-safe reading

PRE enriched traces show that handoff summaries and decision-status fields were missing or incomplete at transfer points. Downstream clarification was associated with missing context rather than only with normal review movement.

This is stronger observability than a Tier 2 candidate readout, but attribution remains scenario-bound until POST comparison.

## What this does not claim

- Not proof that handoff always failed — context may exist outside observable fields.
- No numeric diagnostic scores or internal methodology.
- Not generated by the public Action Preview.
