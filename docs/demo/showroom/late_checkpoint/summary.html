POST

Checkpoints move earlier or become more explicit. Corrections are surfaced before late-stage consolidation, when they are cheaper to address.

What changed

Mandatory pass-through at agreed gates — especially before finalize — reduced skip-ahead paths in trace narratives. Late-stage recovery work appears less often as the default correction mode.
