Overall reading

What is happening

The workflow appears to move forward, but responsibility and context do not always travel cleanly between stages. Downstream review reopens questions that should have been resolved at handoff.

Why it matters

Handoff ambiguity is a coordination pathology — not a single failed task. Public-safe reading focuses on whether context travels across stage boundaries.

Pilot lens

A useful controlled-pilot question: do handoffs include enough shared context that the receiving actor can continue without reopening settled decisions?

PRE / POST / DELTA

PRE

Handoffs are mostly implicit. The receiving stage must reconstruct context and ownership from prior messages.

POST

Handoff points include clearer ownership and short recaps. The receiving stage has more context before taking action.

DELTA
