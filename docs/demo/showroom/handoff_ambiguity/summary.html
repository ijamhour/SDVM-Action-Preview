Remaining friction

Handoffs are implicit. Work moves forward, but downstream actors repeatedly re-open context and decisions that should have been settled earlier.

POST
Improved clarity

Handoff points include explicit ownership and short recaps. Receiving actors have clearer context about decisions, constraints, and open items.

DELTA
Public-safe reading

Fewer ambiguous transfers and less downstream re-clarification. Remaining issue: capture consistency and alignment between the workflow map and lived behavior.

What changed

Handoff contracts and map alignment improved together. Transfers that previously looked unexplained are easier to read as expected paths or documented exceptions. Evidence narratives show less re-orientation immediately after stage boundaries.

What remains unresolved

  • Some transitions may still lack explicit contracts or consistent logging.
  • Map updates alone do not fix behavioral drift if teams bypass agreed paths.
  • Evidence readiness for PRE/POST comparison depends on comparable capture across windows.
