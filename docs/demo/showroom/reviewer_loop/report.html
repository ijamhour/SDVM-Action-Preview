Overall reading

What is happening

The final output may be acceptable, but review effort becomes repetitive. The reviewer repeatedly asks for clarification because earlier stages do not preserve enough decision context.

Why it matters

A reviewer loop is repeated rework visible as a coordination pattern — not a judgment on reviewer thoroughness.

Pilot lens

Ask whether upstream work arrives at review with enough context to decide once, not whether review exists at all.
